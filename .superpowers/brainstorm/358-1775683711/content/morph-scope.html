What morphs with the music?

The app already samples cover art color and shifts mood vars. How far does the morph go?

A

Full palette shift

Accent color, glow color, border tints, and chip colors all derive from the current track's cover art. The whole UI hue shifts per-album. Already partially in place — we push it to every surface.

B

Living backdrop

The app background has a slow-moving ambient gradient — blobs of the track's extracted colors drifting behind frosted glass panels. Like album art dissolved into the room itself.

C

Player bar morphs hardest

The player bar is the most reactive surface. Seek bar glow color, play button ring, visualizer colors — all derived from the track. The bar is the "heartbeat" of the current music.

D

Analog grain layer

A subtle CSS noise/grain overlay on dark surfaces — intensity shifts slightly between tracks. Warm and alive. Doesn't move fast, but it's always there like tape hiss.
