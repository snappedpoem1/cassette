How alive should it feel?

The blend has motion in it — but there's a wide range. Where's your sweet spot?

A

Restrained — motion you feel, not see

Transitions are smooth and 150–200ms. The seek bar has a soft glow when playing. The play button has a barely-there pulse ring. Nothing bounces or breathes visibly. Feels premium, not flashy.

B

Present — you notice it, it doesn't distract

Seek bar glows and has a moving shimmer while playing. Play button has a visible breathing ring at ~3s cycle. Cards lift on hover with a real shadow. Cover art cross-fades on track change. Feels alive.

C

Expressive — it's part of the personality

Waveform/visualizer is prominent and colorful. Track transitions have a beat-sync flash. Hover states have glow trails. The app visibly reacts to the music. Cinematic + club energy turned up.

Note

The app already has a low_motion setting wired in — whatever we design will respect it. This question is about the default experience.
