04 — Living Backdrop & Grain System
Animated color blobs
Three ::before pseudo-element blobs on .app-shell drift slowly using @keyframes. Their colors are set from --mood-accent-rgb inline — when the track changes, the blob colors transition over ~500ms. Behind backdrop-filter: blur glass panels. You're looking at a live version of this right now on this page.
Analog grain overlay
A CSS ::after on body with an SVG feTurbulence noise filter at ~3.5% opacity. Always present, never moves, never distracts. Feels like the surface has texture — like a physical piece of gear rather than a flat screen. Present on this page too.
